About Us

All Pakistan Cables and Conductors Manufacturers Association (APCCMA) was incorporated in 2010. APCCMA targets to protect and promote the interest of all persons dealing with Cables & Conductors Manufacturing. Its further objective is to protect and promote the trade and to secure cooperation among all manufacturers of the cable industry for improving working and products in the country. APCCMA represents the sector before federal and provincial governments or other authorities on behalf of the industry, providing assistance, information, and advice to support the promotion of fair market trade and industry practices. As the leading voice of the sector, APCCMA is committed to promoting collaboration, advocating for industry interests, and driving sustainable growth. Its steadfast dedication aligns with Pakistan's economic goals and development ambitions, establishing APCCMA as a vital force for prosperity and success in the Cable and Conductors sector.
